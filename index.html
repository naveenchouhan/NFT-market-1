What is NFT

An NFT (non-fungible token) is a unique digital item stored on a blockchain. NFTs can represent almost anything, and serve as a digital record of ownership.

How to Buy NFT

In order to buy an NFT, you'll need a crypto wallet and cryptocurrency. Using OpenSea, you can buy items listed for sale instantly, bid in auctions, or make offers on any NFTs.

What is Blockchain

A blockchain is a digitally distributed ledger that records transactions and information across a decentralized network. There are different types of blockchains. OpenSea is compatible with the Ethereum, Polygon, Klaytn, Arbitrum, Optimism, Avalanche, BNB Chain, and Solana blockchains.
